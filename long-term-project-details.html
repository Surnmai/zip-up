long term projects

The secretariat hopes to have a training facility that will focus on building the talents of persons with disability with special slots for orphans. This facility will consist of five departments. Department of Robotics, department of arts and Design, department of music and drama, and Department of IT. Department of Robotics Robotics is an interdisciplinary branch of computer science and engineering. Robotics involves the design, construction, operation, and use of robots. The goal of robotics is to design machines that can help and assist humans. The department will focus on designs that will best solve problems within the African community, especially in the areas of agriculture and household maintenance, and build machines that will be instrumental in aiding persons with disabilities. The department will partner with existing companies and organizations for exchange programs and expertise in various fields to assist in teaching, learning, and inventing new technological ideas. ARTS AND DESIGN Art and Design are about fostering creativity and imagination by building on their knowledge, skills, and understanding of materials and processes. Arts and Design Covering a vast range of skills and talents, art and design is a diverse subject that will help you turn your creative passion into a money-making career. Specific areas of study will be determined by the status quo at the time of commencement. DEPARTMENT OF MUSIC AND DRAMA This department will focus on building talents for the entertainment industry. Currently, the entertainment industry has very few persons with Disability in the system that has made it. Seven in ten people will not able to name three talents with disability in Ghana. The department will train people in instruments, music, dramatic art, and dance. Training will not end on campus; the department will connect students with industry players to have joint and independent projects.
